Johan "N0tail" Sundstein

A tribute to one of my favorite DOTA 2 player (I played him once in Europe Server)

A brief overview of N0tail's carrer:

  • 1993 - Born in Denmark
  • 2012 - Begins his professional Dota 2 career with various teams
  • 2015 - Joins Team Secret and achieves succes in several tournaments
  • 2016 - Forms team OG (Original Gangster) and leads them to multiple Dota 2 Major championships
  • 2018 - Wins The International 2018 with OG, becoming the first two-time TI champion
  • 2019 - Defends the title at the International 2019, solidifying his legacy as one of the greatest Dota 2 players of all time
  • 2021 - Continues to compete at the highest level, showcasing his skill and dedication to the game

"N0tail's versability, innovative strategies, and leadership have made him an icon in the DOTA 2 community."
